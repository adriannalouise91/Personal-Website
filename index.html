About Me


Welcome to my digital battlefield, where I bravely share my experiences, insights, and tips to instill courage in those who feel lost in a vast and ever-changing industry. Within these virtual pages, you will read about my own trials and tribulations, as well as effective strategies for breaking through limiting beliefs, one line of code at a time.

As I began my transition into tech, I was fully aware that I was entering a world driven by innovation, empowerment, and endless learning. For me, coding is not only a form of creative expression but also a powerful tool that allows me to question existing norms and continuously break down barriers I never thought possible for myself.

I invite you to join me on this journey, as I hope to inspire those who are afraid to take those initial steps or are lost in the process. Together, we will explore the ever-evolving world of development, where each line of code we write serves as a bold statement of our unwavering ambition and determination.

So, who am I? My name is Adrianna, and I am a self-taught web developer from Montana. I made the transition into the tech industry from Real Estate in 2021. While I will always have a fondness for the scrappy world of Real Estate, I felt unfulfilled. Computers have always been a natural interest for me since a young age. However, I used to believe that Software Engineering was a field reserved only for the most intelligent minds. Nevertheless, I took the leap anyway.

Throughout these last two years, I've encountered setbacks, questioned my chosen path, and grappled with self-doubt. But rest assured, I'm here to show you that if I can navigate this path, so can you. Join me as I fearlessly pursue my goals and document them each step of the way.
